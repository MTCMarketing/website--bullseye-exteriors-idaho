FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our exterior services, process, and what to expect when you choose Bullseye Exteriors for your home improvement project.

Most residential roofing projects take between 2-5 days, depending on the size and complexity of your roof. Complete tear-off and replacement projects typically take 3-5 days, while smaller repairs can often be completed in a single day. We provide a detailed timeline during your free consultation.

For Idaho's varied climate, we recommend fiber cement siding or vinyl siding. Fiber cement offers excellent durability and resistance to temperature extremes, while modern vinyl siding provides great insulation and requires minimal maintenance. Both options withstand Idaho's weather conditions beautifully.

Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make your exterior projects more affordable. We work with several lending partners to provide competitive rates and payment plans. During your consultation, we can discuss financing options that fit your budget.

We offer comprehensive warranties on all our work. This includes a 10-year workmanship warranty on installations, plus manufacturer warranties on materials (which often range from 25-50 years depending on the product). All warranties are provided in writing for your peace of mind.

During our free inspection, we assess several factors: roof age, extent of damage, shingle condition, and underlying decking. Generally, if your roof is over 20 years old, has multiple leaks, or shows widespread wear, replacement is often more cost-effective. We'll provide honest recommendations based on your roof's condition.

Our free, no-obligation estimate includes a thorough inspection of your exterior, detailed project scope, material recommendations, timeline estimate, and complete cost breakdown. We take the time to answer all your questions and ensure you have all the information needed to make an informed decision.

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate with local building departments for inspections. As licensed professionals, we ensure all work meets or exceeds local building codes. This service is included in our project management at no additional cost to you.

Project start times vary by season, but we typically schedule projects 2-4 weeks after contract signing. During peak season (spring/summer), lead times may be slightly longer. We provide a firm start date in your contract and keep you updated on any scheduling changes.

Our siding installation includes premium underlayment for added insulation and moisture protection, professional flashing around all openings, and expert attention to detail in corners and trim work. We use specialized techniques that ensure your siding will withstand Idaho's climate for decades.

Yes, Bullseye Exteriors is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect your home and our team. We'd be happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.
